# How to Edit the OM Computers Website

Everything on the site is edited from one place: the **admin panel**.
No code, no GitHub, no technical steps.

---

## Logging in

1. Go to **https://your-domain.com/admin**
   (bookmark this — it is intentionally not linked anywhere on the site, so
   only you can find it).
2. Enter your **admin password**.
3. You are in. The top menu has: **Dashboard · Blog Posts · Price List ·
   Site Settings**.

Every change you save appears on the live website **immediately**.

---

## Blog Posts

- **Blog Posts → New post** to write a new article.
- Fill in the **Title**, a short **Excerpt** (shown on the blog listing),
  and **Tags** (comma-separated, e.g. `CCTV, Tips, Security`).
- Write the article in the **Post body** box using simple Markdown:
  - `**bold text**` → **bold**
  - `*italic text*` → *italic*
  - `## Sub Heading` → a section heading
  - `- item` → a bullet point
  - `[link text](https://example.com)` → a link
  - `| a | b |` table rows → a table
- **Cover image:** click *Choose file* to upload one (max 5 MB).
- **Inline images:** scroll to *Insert an image into the post*, upload, then
  copy the little `![...](...)` snippet it gives you into the body where you
  want the picture.
- Tick **Published** when it is ready. Leave it unticked to keep it as a
  hidden draft.
- Click **Save post**.

To edit or delete a post later: **Blog Posts** → click its title (or the
**Delete** button).

---

## Price List

- **Price List** shows your categories (Processors, Laptops, Storage, …).
- Click **Add category** to create a new one.
- Click **Edit products** on a category to:
  - change a product's name, brand, specs, price, or stock status
  - upload a product photo
  - **Add a product** at the bottom
  - **Delete** a product
- Every change saves to the live site instantly.

---

## Site Settings

**Site Settings** controls the details shown in the header, footer, and
contact page across the whole site:

- Phone number
- WhatsApp number (digits only, with country code — e.g. `919876543210`)
- Email address
- Full address
- Google Maps embed URL (Google Maps → Share → *Embed a map* → copy the
  `src` address)
- Opening hours

Edit, then **Save settings** — the whole site updates at once.

---

## Good to know

- **Forgot to log out?** Sessions expire after 7 days. To log out manually,
  click **Log out** in the top-right.
- **Your images are safe** — they are stored in the website's database, not
  as loose files, so nothing is lost when the site updates.
- **Made a mistake?** Just edit the item again and save; there is no
  "publish" delay.
- **Changing your password** is done by your developer/host in the Hostinger
  control panel (the `ADMIN_PASSWORD` setting).
